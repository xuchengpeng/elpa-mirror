1. Screenshots


This is a low contrast grayish colour theme derived from [Zenburn] but
with inverted colours.  It is tested to work on GNU Emacs 24 and 23.

To use it, use the built-in defpackage machinery like load-theme and the
like.


[Zenburn] <http://github.com/bbatsov/zenburn-emacs>
